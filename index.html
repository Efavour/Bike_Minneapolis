BIKE MPLS

Go For A Ride

Experience Minneapolis like never before by exploring the city on two wheels. From the picturesque Chain of Lakes to the historic Stone Arch Bridge, our bike-friendly city invites you to discover its vibrant neighborhoods, cultural gems, and scenic parks effortlessly. With an extensive network of bike trails and a convenient bike-sharing program, Minneapolis is yours to explore at your own pace. Grab a helmet, hop on a bike, and pedal your way through the heart of this dynamic city, where history, modernity, and nature converge for an unforgettable adventure.

See The City

Thanks to a large network of trails and paths, cycling is a popular way to get around in the Twin Cities. Go for an evening loop around Bde Maka Ska, tour Minneapolis’ downtown riverfront or cruise along on the tree-lined bike lanes of Summit Avenue in St. Paul.

Mountain Biking

Exploring Minnesota’s natural beauty on two wheels is easy. With 100-plus miles of mountain bike trails, the Duluth Traverse winds through forested hills and vibrant meadows. The red-dirt trails of Cuyuna beckon cyclists to its 800 acres. For trails suited to every level of ridership, head to Lebanon Hills just south of St. Paul in Eagan.

Explore Parks

Discovering Minneapolis parks is a delightful adventure, unveiling the city's natural charm and recreational treasures. From the cascading beauty of Minnehaha Regional Park to the serene Chain of Lakes and the versatile Theodore Wirth Park, the city's green spaces offer diverse experiences. Whether biking along scenic trails, enjoying lakeside tranquility, or partaking in community events, Minneapolis parks provide a refreshing escape and a vibrant tapestry of outdoor enjoyment.

Stone Arch Bridge

The Stone Arch Bridge in Minneapolis, a historic masterpiece of 1883 railroad construction, now a picturesque pedestrian pathway, spans the mighty Mississippi River, offering breathtaking views of the city skyline and St. Anthony Falls while serving as a captivating link between Minneapolis's industrial past and vibrant present.

Bde Maka Ska

Lake Bde Maka Ska, nestled in the heart of Minneapolis, is a serene urban oasis, offering locals and visitors alike a picturesque setting for recreational activities with its sparkling waters, scenic trails, and vibrant lakeside atmosphere.

Theodore Wirth

The expansive Theodore Wirth Park in Minneapolis invites outdoor enthusiasts to explore its diverse landscapes, from lush meadows and winding trails to winter-ready slopes, providing a year-round haven for recreation and relaxation.
